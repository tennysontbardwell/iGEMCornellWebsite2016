Applications for 2016 are now closed.

Thank you for your interest in Cornell iGEM. We cannot stress enough the importance of new members to this team’s success. Our project each year grows from the ideas and energy of our new teammates. We welcome all majors: anyone who is interested in what we do!

Generally our schedule stays consistent from year to year:

  • Spring: brainstorm next project and new member training (2 credits, 6-8 hrs/week)
  • Summer: on-campus project work (20+ hrs/week) All new team members are expected to stay in Ithaca over the summer
  • Fall: finish project and compete at iGEM jamborees (3-4 credits, 12-16 hrs/week)
We ask applicants to complete an application form and submit a resume. We will also look at any provided supplemental materials and conduct interviews before deciding on new members.

If you would like to learn more, please feel free to email us at cornelligem@gmail.com or visit our Facebook page.
