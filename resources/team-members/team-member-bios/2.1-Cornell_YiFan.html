Yi Fan is a junior majoring in biological sciences with a strong passion for organic chemistry and physics. His research interest lies in the regulatory and catalytic mechanisms of vesicular trafficking at the Golgi Apparatus. He would like to make a difference in the lives of patients while also contributing to the development of more effective treatments at the frontier of science and medicine. Fun fact: he used to own pet silkworms.
